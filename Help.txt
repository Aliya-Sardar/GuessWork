Step 1:
	To play the game, first enter your name. 
	Then the 'Play' button will appear.

Step 2:
	Press the 'Play' button.

Step 3:
	A drawing will start and the player will have to guess it. 
	On the wrong guess, the game will end.

Step 4:
	Check your score.

Step 5:
	Beat your friend's highscore!!!
